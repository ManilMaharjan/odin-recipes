Chicken Momo

Description

Chicken momo is a popular Nepali dumpling filled with seasoned minced chicken and wrapped in a soft flour dough. It is usually steamed and served with spicy tomato achar.

This dish is loved for its juicy filling and simple preparation, making it perfect for family meals or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 250g minced chicken
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Water (as needed)

Steps

  1. Mix flour with water and knead into a soft dough.
  2. In a bowl, combine chicken, onion, garlic, ginger, salt, and oil.
  3. Roll the dough into small circles.
  4. Place filling in the center and fold to seal.

  6. Serve hot with tomato achar.
