Dal Bhat

Description

Dal Bhat is a traditional Nepali meal consisting of lentil soup and rice, served with vegetables and pickles.

It is nutritious, filling, and eaten daily in many Nepali households.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Lentils (dal)
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Vegetables
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Cook rice until soft.
  2. Boil lentils with turmeric and salt.
  3. Prepare vegetables separately.
  4. Serve rice with dal and vegetables.
