Chicken Chowmein

Description

Chicken chowmein is a popular noodle dish made with stir-fried noodles, vegetables, and seasoned chicken.

It is quick to prepare and perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Chicken (sliced)
  • Onion
  • Cabbage
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil noodles and drain.
  2. Stir-fry chicken until cooked.
  3. Add vegetables and cook briefly.
  4. Add noodles and sauces.
  5. Mix well and serve hot.
